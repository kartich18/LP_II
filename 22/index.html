Welcome to PICT

Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT) is a premier engineering institute established in 1983. The institute is approved by AICTE and affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education contributing to the development of competent professionals for the industry and society.

Mission

To provide quality technical education and prepare competitive, innovative and entrepreneurial professionals ready to contribute to the advancement of the industry and society.
